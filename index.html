About Us

Who We Are

ECOYST is a forward-thinking, eco-driven brand founded in 2025 with a mission to transform everyday food waste into powerful, natural compost. We are dedicated to enriching soil, supporting plant health, and promoting sustainable living.

Our Story

ECOYST began with a simple realization: while food waste cannot be entirely eliminated, it can be transformed into something valuable. Instead of letting organic matter pollute landfills, we return it to the earth in its purest, most useful form — nutrient-rich compost produced via machine-assisted, chemical-free decomposition.

Our Commitment to Quality

  • Controlled machine processing and strict quality checks
  • Zero chemical additives and zero contamination
  • Batch-wise testing to ensure consistent nutrient value

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We reduce food waste by converting household food scraps into nutrient-rich organic compost, promote circular-economy practices, and support soil restoration.

Made With Hygiene & Care

Every step is designed to maintain clean handling, proper temperatures, and secure packaging — producing a safe, hygienic product suitable for homes, nurseries, and farms.

Our Vision

To create a sustainable world where organic waste becomes a resource, not a burden.

Our Mission

Since food waste cannot be completely stopped, our mission is to repurpose it responsibly — reduce waste, nourish the earth, and encourage greener lifestyles through natural composting solutions.

USP

ECOYST is the first premium, high-efficacy compost that directly converts household food scraps into powerful plant nutrition — enabling consumers to participate actively in the circular economy.

About the Product

What Is ECOYST Compost?

ECOYST compost is a 100% natural, nutrient-rich organic compost created from carefully selected food waste. Designed to enhance soil health and boost plant growth without any chemicals.

How Our Compost Is Made

  1. Collection: We collect safe, biodegradable food scraps from trusted sources.
  2. Classification: Waste is carefully sorted to remove non-organic items.
  3. Machine Composting: Sorted scraps are processed in our custom composting machines that maintain ideal temperatures and moisture for efficient decomposition, pathogen destruction, and odor control.
  4. Hygienic Packing: Compost is sifted, quality-tested, and packed in clean, controlled conditions to preserve freshness and nutrients.

Why ECOYST Is Better

  • 100% natural — no chemicals or artificial additives
  • Machine-controlled for uniform quality
  • Made from household food waste — supports waste reduction
  • Hygienically processed and packaged

Terms & Conditions — Ecoyst Organic Compost

Quality & Production Standards

Ecoyst ensures compliance with quality norms consistent with the Fertiliser (Control) Order, 1985 (FCO) and other applicable guidelines.

Physical Properties

  • Final compost should be black or brown, crumbly, with an earthy odor.
  • Moisture content during composting: 50–60% (final product should not be completely dry).

Nutrient Content

Total nutrient content (N, P, K) should generally be more than 1% each. C/N ratio should be approximately 15–20.

Production Process

Approved composting methods (e.g., windrow, in-vessel) must maintain required temperature ranges (e.g., 55°C–77°C) for specified durations to ensure destruction of pathogens and weed seeds.

Heavy Metal Limits

The product must not exceed permissible limits for heavy metals (Arsenic, Cadmium, Chromium, Copper, Lead, etc.). Ecoyst conducts regular checks to ensure safety.

Prohibited Substances

Ecoyst compost does not contain synthetic fertilizers, chemical hormones, synthetic growth promoters, or other prohibited substances.

Terms for Beneficiaries / Farmers

Government schemes (e.g., PKVY, MOVCD) have scheme-specific eligibility, cluster formation, certification and documentation requirements. Beneficiaries must follow NPOP/PGS guidelines where applicable.

Terms for Manufacturers / Suppliers

  • Licensing: Valid certificate of manufacture under FCO, 1985.
  • Quality Control: Laboratory facilities for testing and batch-wise monitoring.
  • Compliance: Non-compliance may lead to penalties, fines, or revocation of permissions.

Disclaimer: Ecoyst Organic Compost is intended for soil enrichment and sustainable agricultural/horticultural use. Eligibility for government schemes is subject to official program rules and verification.
